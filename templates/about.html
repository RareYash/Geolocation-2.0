About City locator

Moving to a new city is exciting — but finding essentials like restaurants, transit, and medical facilities can take days. City locator puts everything you need in one beautiful, ad-free interface.

Currently built for Nashik, Maharashtra, our platform uses machine learning to rank places based on proximity, budget, and popularity.
